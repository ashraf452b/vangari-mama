How Vangari Mama Works

Your one-stop platform to sell scrap and find treasures.

For Sellers (Users who want to sell scrap)

  1. Create an Account: Register yourself as a "User".
  2. Post Your Scrap: Click on "Create Post" from your dashboard. Fill in the details like trash type, quantity, location, asking price, and your contact number.
  3. Receive Offers: Collectors will see your post and make offers based on your asking price.
  4. Accept/Reject Offers: You will see all offers on your dashboard. You can choose to accept the best offer.
  5. Complete the Deal: Once you accept an offer, the collector will contact you to arrange pickup and payment.

For Collectors (Users who want to buy scrap)

  1. Create an Account: Register yourself as a "Collector".
  2. Browse Available Posts: From your dashboard or the "Available Posts" page, you can see all scrap items available for pickup.
  3. Make an Offer: View the details of a post you are interested in. You can then make a negotiation by proposing a final weight and price.
  4. Wait for Acceptance: The seller will be notified of your offer. If they accept, the post will be marked as yours.
  5. Collect and Pay: Contact the seller using the provided phone number to schedule the pickup and complete the payment.
